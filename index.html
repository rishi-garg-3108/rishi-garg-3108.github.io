About Me

I am an AI Engineer and Data Scientist with 2+ years of experience and a master’s degree in Computer Science (AI) from the University of Paderborn, Germany. I have worked as a research assistant at Fraunhofer IEM, where I contributed on real-world, industry-driven projects and built end-to-end project and POC as part of the GenAI Incubator. I have been involved in scientific research projects at the university, with a focus on efficient and explainable machine learning. My master’s thesis explores lightweight, explainable models for detecting LLM-generated text, aligning with my broader interests in deep learning, model efficiency, and generative AI.

Previously, I was a founding member of Masterji Classes, an edtech startup, where I managed core operations, taught Python and data structures to 100+ students, and worked as an Data Scientist developing machine learning solutions for student enrollment prediction. When I’m not working, you’ll find me listening to tech podcasts or enjoying nature walks.
