About Me

Hi, I’m **Rishi Garg**—a master’s candidate in Computer Science (AI) and student researcher in Fraunhofer IEM’s GenAI Incubator, where I build proof-of-concepts in generative AI. My thesis focuses on training and optimising lightweight models to detect LLM-generated text, reflecting my broader interests in deep learning, model efficiency, and GenAI. Previously, I was the founding member of **Masterji Classes**, an edtech startup, teaching Python and data structures to 100 + students. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me listening to tech podcasts, experimenting with Indian recipes, or nature walking.
