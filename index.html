About Me

I am an AI Engineer with a master’s degree in Computer Science (AI) from the University of Paderborn. I have worked as a research assistant at Fraunhofer IEM, where I contributed as an AI engineer on real-world, industry-driven projects and built proof-of-concepts as part of the GenAI Incubator. My master’s thesis focuses on training and optimising lightweight, explainable models for detecting LLM-generated text, reflecting my broader interests in deep learning, model efficiency, and generative AI.

Previously, I was a founding member of Masterji Classes, an edtech startup, where I managed core operations, taught Python and data structures to 100+ students, and worked as an ML engineer developing machine learning solutions for student enrollment prediction. When I’m not working, you’ll find me listening to tech podcasts or enjoying nature walks.
